welcome to Vondelkwartier

Nestled at the edge of Haarlem , Vondelkwartier is a fresh and vibrant addition to our historic city. Built with a focus on modern living, green spaces, and family-friendly amenities, our neighborhood offers a unique blend of contemporary comfort and natural beauty.

Nature at Your Doorstep

For nature enthusiasts, Vondelkwartier is a paradise waiting to be explored. We're surrounded by a beautiful park and several green spaces, offering serene escapes just steps away from your front door. Whether it's a leisurely walk or a peaceful picnic, you'll find tranquility in our natural surroundings.

Playgrounds for Kids

Families with children will love Vondelkwartier's commitment to providing safe and enjoyable spaces for kids to play. Our neighborhood features well-maintained playgrounds where children can run, swing, and explore to their hearts' content.
